Delivery Methods and Timing

This delivery policy explains the methods and timing of delivery for services acquired through the CIDB EXPATRATE Platform. Please read through this policy carefully to understand how we manage service delivery and what to expect after your purchase.

Delivery Methods

Services purchased on the CIDB EXPATRATE Platform are delivered electronically via online.

Delivery Timing

The timing of service delivery varies depending on the type of service purchased:
1. Expatrate Application Services: Application is registered immediately upon order confirmation and paid.
2. JKSE Endorsement Services: JKSE Endorsement will be confirm after the endorsement is complete in JKSE.

Accessing Purchased Services

Ensure you can access the purchased services within 14 days from delivery. Contact CIDB EXPATRATE within 48 hours in case of access issues.
